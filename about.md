## About Retronian

👾 **Retronian** is a project dedicated to retro gaming handhelds, custom operating systems, and 3D printed accessories.

We believe retro gaming should be accessible, portable, and fun. Our mission is to build the best custom OS experience across all handheld devices — **One OS, All Handhelds.**

### What We Do

- **Custom OS Development** — Building a unified operating system for retro gaming handhelds
- **Hardware Reviews** — In-depth analysis of handheld consoles and their SoCs
- **3D Printed Accessories** — Designing and sharing printable accessories for popular handhelds
- **Guides & Tutorials** — Helping the community get the most out of their devices
